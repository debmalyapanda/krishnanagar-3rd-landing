Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our interior design consultation, timelines, material sourcing, and pricing.

What is the typical timeline for a complete villa interior project?
+

Our standard luxury villa project ranges between 60 to 90 days from design sign-off to white-glove handover. Modular kitchens and single rooms can be executed within 30 to 45 days.

Do you offer photorealistic 3D VR walkthroughs before starting work?
+

Yes! Every project includes complete 3D photorealistic renderings and VR spatial walkthroughs so you can visualize textures, lighting, and layout before construction begins.

Where are your materials and furniture components sourced from?
+

We partner with premier Italian, German, and domestic ateliers for marbles, soft-close hardware, custom veneers, and luxury acoustic wall panels.

What structural warranty and post-handover support do you provide?
+

We provide a comprehensive 10-year structural warranty on all built-in furniture and woodwork, accompanied by annual complimentary maintenance checkups.
